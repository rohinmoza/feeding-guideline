Infant Feeding Guideline

Postoperative & Non-Surgical Populations

Inclusion Criteria
Infants < 90 days meeting ≥ 1 of the following:
Single ventricle physiology LVOT obstruction Systemic-to-pulmonary shunt / PDA stent Large unrepaired PDA Truncus / AP window MAPCAs Weight < 2.5 kg
Exclusion Criteria
sVAD Prematurity < 36 wks GA Recurrent hematochezia
Criteria for Feeding
All criteria must be met to initiate feeds:
✓
VIS score < 5 (excluding Milrinone)
✓
Arterial lactate < 3
✓
No severe persistent ileus
✓
No escalating respiratory support beyond HFNC, and if on CPAP, pressure ≤ 7
✓
No imminent procedure that would require stopping or holding feeds
Advancement Pathway
1
Initiate TPN / SMOF
Goal 60 kcal/kg/day
2
Start Enteral Feeds at 20 mL/kg/day
BM / DBM / Pepticate 20 kcal/oz
q3h bolus · PO or PO/NG over 1 hr
3
Advance 20 mL/kg/day q24h to 80 mL/kg/day
20 → 40 → 60 → 80 mL/kg/day
Q24H VOLUME INCREASES
⚑ When ordering the increase to 80: simultaneously order fortification to 22 kcal/oz. Fortified formula will arrive ~12h later for the evening feed.
4
Fortify to 22 kcal/oz
Arrives ~12h after reaching 80 (PM feed)
CALORIC DENSITY INCREASE
5
Advance 20 mL/kg/day q12h to 120 mL/kg/day
100 → 120 mL/kg/day
Q12H VOLUME INCREASES
6
Complete a full day at 120 mL/kg/day → Discontinue TPN
Keep SMOF at 1.5 g/kg/day
7
Advance to 140 mL/kg/day
⚑ When ordering the increase to 140: simultaneously order fortification to 24 kcal/oz. Fortified formula will arrive ~12h later for the evening feed.
8
Fortify to 24 kcal/oz
Arrives ~12h after reaching 140 (PM feed)
CALORIC DENSITY INCREASE
9
Advance to 150 mL/kg/day → Discontinue SMOF
12–24h later
✓
Goal: 150 mL/kg/day at 24 kcal/oz
Full enteral nutrition achieved
* If feeding intolerance at any step below goal:
  • Increase bolus duration in 30-min increments (max 2 hrs)
  • If persistent, return to previously tolerated step (volume or caloric density)
★ Exception at Goal

At goal (150 mL/kg/day, 24 kcal/oz): If persistent intolerance, reduce volume to 140 mL/kg/day and fortify to 26 kcal/oz.
